# Masda-aw — Digital Commons & Archive

Official website of **Masda-aw** (*curiosity* in Ilocano) — a pioneering community engagement art project connecting indigenous Kalinga weavers and artisans with urban creatives, revitalizing traditional crafts through collaborative innovation, creating sustainable livelihoods, and preserving cultural heritage.

**Kalinga × Manila · Since August 2025 · #comefromcuriosity**

🌐 Live at [masda-aw.org](https://masda-aw.org)

## About this site

This site is hand-coded static HTML/CSS — no templates, no frameworks, no database. Built to load fast on Philippine mobile data and to endure as a permanent public archive of the project.

Made possible through the Direct Aid Program of the Australian Embassy in the Philippines, the patronage of Mercedes Zobel, and the support of the Philippine Fiber Industry Development Authority (PhilFIDA).
